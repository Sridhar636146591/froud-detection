About Reverse Verification

This system sends verification requests directly to:

  • Employers - To confirm employment and income
  • Banks - To verify account details and transactions
  • Schools/Universities - To verify educational claims
  • Local Authorities - To confirm address and residency
Instead of trusting user-submitted documents, we verify directly from the source.
